Lebanon Nights Dessert

Mohallabia

Lebanon Nights Dessert is one of the most famous types of Levantine sweets. It is characterized by a delicious taste and is easy to make at home. Here is how to make Lebanon Night's Dessert:

Ingredients:

a cup of fine semolina.
4 cups of liquid milk.
a quarter cup of sugar.
3 pieces of mastic.
a teaspoon of rose water.
a cup of cream.
half a cup of ground pistachio.
Syrup.

How to prepare:

1-Bring a pot and put it on the fire, then put the mastic grains in it and leave them until they melt.
2-Add semolina, sugar, and milk while it is cold to the pot while continuing to stir.
3-Leave the mixture on low heat with constant stirring until it becomes thick.
4-Remove the pot from the heat and add the rose water to the mixture, mixing it well.
5-Pour Lebanon Nights into a medium mold, preferably with a layer of medium thickness.
6-Put the mold in the refrigerator until it cools down.
7-Take the mold out of the refrigerator, then put the cream on the entire face.
8-Add the pistachios to cover the cream.
9-Serve Lebanon Nights cold, with the addition of syrup as desired.
