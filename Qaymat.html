Qaymat

Lebanon Nights Dessert

Mohallabia

Qaymat is one of the oriental sweets loved by all Arab families and is distinguished from other sweets by its simplicity of preparation, lack of ingredients, and delicious taste. Learn about the best way to make Qaymat:

Ingredients:

One cup of flour.
a quarter teaspoon of salt.
One Cup of water
One teaspoon of yeast
One tablespoon of sugar
One tablespoon of white starch
a tablespoon of oil
Two cups of pre-prepared syrup
frying oil.

How to prepare:

1-Start by mixing the dry ingredients together (flour, starch, and salt).
2-Mix the yeast, sugar, and a little warm water together, set aside for 10 minutes, and then add to the dry ingredients.
3-Add the rest of the ingredients together and knead them well until you get a soft and homogeneous dough.
4-Leave the dough for half an hour until it ferments well.
5-Heat the frying oil well.
6-Make small balls from the dough; you can use cream bags or a spoon, and then place them in the hot oil.
7-When you get the golden color of the Qaymat, it is removed from the oil and left in the strainer for a while, then placed in the syrup for a few minutes, then placed in the serving dish.
