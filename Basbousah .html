Basbousah

This recipe is one of the famous Arabic sweets; its ingredients are simple and inexpensive, and it is available in most homes. Basbousah is one of the most delicious sweets. Learn how to prepare it in detail.

Ingredients :

Two cups of semolina
One cup of sugar
One cup of milk powder
quarter cup of Vegetarian oil
A third of a cup of water
160 g of cream
A teaspoon and a half of baking powder
397 g of sweetened condensed milk

How to prepare:

1.In a bowl, put semolina, sugar, milk, oil, water, cream, and baking powder.
2.Then mix the ingredients until you get a mixed basbousa mixture.
3.Pour the mixture into an oiled tray.
4.Put the tray into a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius until the mixture is ripe and golden.
5.Water the basbousah with condensed milk and leave it until it absorbs the milk completely.
6.Cut the basbousah after it cools down and serve it on a plate. Health and wellness!
