Mohallabia

Mohallabia is one of the most famous desserts in the Arab world, and it is a favorite among children and adults. It is distinguished by its ease of preparation and its delicious and pleasant taste.

Ingredients

Four cups of liquid milk.
Four tablespoons of starch
Half cup of sugar.
big spoon of flower water.

How to prepare:

1-Put three and a half cups of milk and sugar in a pot over high heat and leave the mixture until it reaches a boil.
2-Dissolve the starch in half a cup of cold milk, add it gradually to the boiling milk, and continue to stir.
3-Leave the mixture on the fire until it reaches the boiling stage, for no less than two minutes.
4-then remove it from the fire and add the blossom water. distribute it into dishes suitable for serving, and sprinkle on the face coconut, pistachios, or almonds.
